Project description

All in the Same boat was one of the first CAAT’s big projects. We counted with the support of many colleagues and friends, and we had 3 different phases: a training course in Porto, Portugal, a Youth Exchange in Bansko, Bulgaria and a Seminar in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. We created the project in response to the “refugee-crisis”, reaction of the host communities and the increasing online “hate speech”. Therefore, this project aimed to empower youth workers and youth leaders to identify and prevent hate speech and other possible problems in the communities regarding integration of minorities and youth. The main aim was to create a sustainable long-term project based on non-formal education and informal learning, with the duration of one year, in order to develop competences in Youth and Youth Workers for the advocacy and mediation of a truly multicultural community/society, focusing in the integration of migrants and ethnic minorities as well as the promotion of no hate speech and anti-discriminatory strategies.

Be International is an organization working since November 2009. We are a small community of around 10 people, excited and eager to share our knowledge and to learn new skills. We are based at the Faculty of Social Studies of Masaryk University in Brno, Czech Republic.

SOS RACISMO has existed since 1990 and proposes a fairer, more egalitarian and intercultural society where everyone, nationals and foreigners of any skin tone, can enjoy the same citizenship rights. We are a non-profit association, having been granted public utility status in 1996.

Youthnet Hellas is a non-governmental, non-profit organisation that seeks, though actions and interventions, the active participation of Youth in local, national, European and international level. It started as a group of young people from all over Greece who decided to channel all their passion and energy, as well as their enriched experience from a long term occupation in the Municipal and Regional Youth Councils, and their knowledge on Youth Policies, European Programmes and Volunteering, into a new NGO.

This project is funded by the National Agency in The Netherlands under European Commission’s Erasmus+ programme.
