Our History

It is great when you find someone that shares your views on human rights and is willing to put their time and effort in improving what they saw as injustice. This happened many years ago with Anna and Andreia. Others have joined and after a few actions together we decided that we would form an organization. Thus, Anna, Andreia, Catia and Tatiana took the lead and registered Stichting CAAT Projects in January 2016. We started our operations in The Hague, together with other organizations, like the The Hague Youth Factory and a couple of other ones. Years later we moved our operational centre to Amsterdam, to the OT301 building. The social actors in CAAT have changed and we now we have integrated other colleagues as Steve and Kim in our board. We have also the pleasure of collaborating with an enthusiastic group of volunteers and friends. Despite the changes in location and team, our core value continues the same – human rights for all. We will keep on building an impressive record of successful projects in the Netherlands and abroad, achieving the goals set in the organizational planning. Moreover, CAAT Projects has also been delivering on the requirements of the EU funded projects and bringing people together from various backgrounds. Our door keeps on being open to new opportunities, projects and collaboration either with organizations or individuals. If you think you share similar values than us, contact us and we would brainstorm together.
